Privacy Policy

PicTailor is provided by jinshuihu.

How the App Works

PicTailor lets you select images, stitch screenshots, capture webpage snapshots, edit results, and export them to your device or system share destinations.

Information We Process

  • Photos and screenshots you choose inside the app
  • Webpage content when you use the webpage snapshot feature or Safari share extension
  • Clipboard URLs when the app returns to the root screen and a valid webpage link is available
  • Diagnostic and crash data used to improve app stability

How Information Is Used

  • To generate stitched images and webpage snapshots locally on your device
  • To save exported images to your photo library or share destinations you choose
  • To investigate crashes and improve reliability

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. If crash reporting or infrastructure providers are used, they are used only to support app operations and diagnostics.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact pictailor.vip@gmail.com.
